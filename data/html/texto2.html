Data

The data has been obtained from Madrid Open Data. This webpage is a space that the Madrid town hall offers in order to:

  • Ease access to public data in a reusable format relating to municipal public management
  • Promote the development of creative tools to attract and serve the citizens of Madrid.
It contains a data catalog with all published datasets and an API to facilitate direct access to the data.

The specific datasets used for the development of this project are:

  • Dataset of monthly acoustic measures
  • Dataset of measurement stations location
